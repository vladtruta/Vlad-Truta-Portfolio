About me

I am an aspiring developer, with a passion for coding, especially when there’s also a little front-end work to be done. I could call myself “full-stack”, however I am not specialized in any particular area, as of now. I am trying to expand my knowledge and find programming-related things which are interesting and worth the time and effort. On top of my priority list, is learning about design philosophies and UI/UX interactions.

Where I am now

Right now, these are the tools and skills I'm most proficient in. I'm constantly trying to evolve and expand my area of knowledge, which means I'm never pleased with how much I know.
